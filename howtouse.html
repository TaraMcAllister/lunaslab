Resources

Here at Luna's Lab we have designed this interactive resource in order to teach young people about the Periodic Table, and elements through the key skill of maths.

The homepage brings the children straight to practice their mathematical skills.

Get the correct answer to the equation on the homepage and learn fun facts about the element with that atomic number! If the answer is incorrect you will be prompted to try again. If the question is too difficult simply click the 'Try a Different Problem' button to be faced with a new challenge.

This site is optimised for iPads, computers and Interactive Smartboards so is the perfect tool for the classroom.

Head over to our Teachers section to find more resources for teaching the Periodic Table - whether the children in your care are at Key Stage 2, 3 or 4 level, we have resources that will fit their needs, and yours.

Enjoy!
