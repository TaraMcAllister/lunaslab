Fun Facts

Oxygen can be found in the air. We need Oxygen in the air or else we couldn't breathe it.

Oxygen is in water.

Oxygen is part of our DNA.

In pre-historic times things used to be much bigger because there was more oxygen in the air. Dragonflies were the size of birds.
