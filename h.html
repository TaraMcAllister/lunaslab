Fun Facts

Hydrogen can make clean energy. One day our cars will use Hydrogen instead of petrol or diesel.

We need Hydrogen to live. It makes up part of our DNA.

About 10% of your body weight is Hydrogen.

The Big Bang created Hydrogen. It also created lithium and helium.
