Resources

We at Luna's Lab know that finding the perfect resources can be hard - as can having the time to find them in the first place. We aim to make your job easier and decrease the time and effort you have to spend scouring the internet for appropriate resources for all of the different levels within your classroom.

While Luna's Lab is aimed at KS2, we understand that young people at KS3 or 4 age might also use it - and be in your classroom learning about the Periodic Table. That's why we have resources for KS2, KS3 and KS4. Below you will find resources sorted by Key Stage and type. PowerPoints, worksheets, banners - we have it all!
