Fun Facts

Pure Chlorine burns your eyes and mouth when breathed in.

Chlorine Gas is why people wore Gas Masks in WW1.

Used to kill germs and bacteria in swimming pools.

Salt we use in our food is called ‘Chlorine Sodium’ – it has chlorine and sodium (two elements) in it. Two elements together are called a compound.
